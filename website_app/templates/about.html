Welcome to Senior Seva Helpline

Senior Seva Helpline is a FREE service to the senior citizens of Belagavi city which helps with

On-boarding for Govt Schemes Home Delivery Services Healthcare connects thru vendor tie-ups

In addition, the Senior Seva Mitra establishes a young buddy tie-up program to provide Companionship & Accompaniment for Elders via

Hiring & training young adults & students to empathize with seniors Adopting age-tech tools to efficiently manage service requests Facilitating ease-of-use and serviceability via a simple phone call

Built to serve our Senior Citizens, Senior Seva Helpline delivers information & services to help ease their Lifestyles and worries!

Our motto is to provide the elderly easy access to their day-to-day needs and be self-reliant. All these services are made possible by

  • Seva Helpline researching, negotiating and signing MOU with partner organizations so that they provide these services at an affordable price & within the specified TAT

Community Support

  • Collaborations with Associations, Corporates, NGOs and Govt agencies to help Spread the word | Reach into semi-urban | Serve the rural population
