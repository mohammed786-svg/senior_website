About Us

Senior Seva Helpline is a FREE service to the senior citizens of Belagavi city. In addition, the Senior Seva Mitra establishes a young buddy tie-up program to provide Companionship & Accompaniment for Elders. Built to serve our Senior Citizens, Senior Seva Helpline delivers information & services to help ease their Lifestyles and worries!
